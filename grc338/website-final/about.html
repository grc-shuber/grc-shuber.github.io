  • Welcome to Fin and Scale!

    At Fin and Scale, we believe in delivering the ocean’s finest to your plate. As a family-owned fish market, our mission is to provide the freshest, sustainably sourced seafood to our community. Every product we offer is carefully selected to ensure top quality, whether it’s wild-caught fish, premium shellfish, or chef-prepared specialties. With years of experience in the seafood industry, we are passionate about connecting you to the best flavors of the sea while promoting responsible fishing practices.

  • Our Commitment to Quality

    Freshness is at the heart of everything we do. We work closely with trusted fisheries and local suppliers to bring you seafood that’s caught and delivered within hours. From the Atlantic to the Pacific, we source a diverse range of fish and shellfish to meet your every need, whether you’re planning a gourmet dinner, a backyard barbecue, or a quick and healthy meal. At Fin and Scale, you can always trust that what you’re buying is not only delicious but also responsibly harvested.

  • More Than a Fish Market

    Fin and Scale is more than a place to shop – it’s a community hub for seafood lovers. Our friendly team is here to share cooking tips, recipe ideas, and expert advice to help you make the most of your purchases. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or just discovering the joys of cooking seafood, we’re here to make your experience memorable. Stop by today and discover why Fin and Scale is your go-to destination for the freshest seafood around!
